Her..

How do I come to terms with the feeling you awaken in me? You appear in every image my closed eyes dare to dream. You are love itself— the pulse behind my prayers. In every supplication, I whisper your name. If ever you wished for death, there would be no answer, for I ask only for your longevity— enough to outlive my own heart. I remain in love. You are love, and all things above its meaning. Because of you, I wish you to remain my sweetest secret, my perfectest words, my passion, my inspiration in human form. Never say my care is less than none. If I wrote how I feel for you, the oceans would run dry. I love you in every minute of every day. O beloved, reside in my eyes— comfortable, eternal, forever yours to stay.
by : Muhammed Dastigeer Khan
